HealthConnect: Appointment Management App

January 2020 â€“ August 2020

HealthConnect is a comprehensive appointment management application designed to streamline healthcare interactions between patients, clerks, and doctors. The app enhances accessibility and communication within the healthcare system through three dedicated modules.

Patient Module

Allows patients to effortlessly book, cancel, and access emergency services through the application. This provides flexibility and convenience for users to manage their healthcare appointments seamlessly.

Clerk Module

Enables clerks to efficiently manage appointment requests. They can accept or reject appointments based on the doctor's schedule. The module also includes a notification feature, allowing clerks to promptly inform patients of the status of their appointments.

Doctor Module

Empowers doctors to view a comprehensive list of patient appointments scheduled for a particular day. Additionally, doctors can issue emergency or important notices to clerks, ensuring effective communication within the healthcare team.
